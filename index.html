About

I am a PhD researcher at the School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University. My research focuses on automated radiology report generation using multimodal deep learning, large language models, and cross-modal attention mechanisms for clinical NLP tasks.

Prior to my PhD, I served as an IT Instructor at Ajou University in Tashkent and held roles in international relations and education management. I hold an MSc in Software Engineering from Wuhan Textile University (CGPA 3.8/4.0) and a BSc in Textile Engineering from TITLI, Tashkent.

I am a recipient of the El-Yurt Umidi Foundation Scholarship (2023) and the ITEC Government of India certificate in Science, Technology and Innovation Policy.

Research Interests

  • Automated Radiology Report Generation (RRG) - multimodal deep learning, vision-language models
  • Large Language Model (LLM) fine-tuning strategies for clinical NLP tasks
  • Cross-modal attention and dynamic memory-augmented Transformer architectures
  • Adaptive scoring mechanisms and evaluation metrics (BLEU, METEOR, ROUGE-L) for medical text
  • Image classification, knowledge distillation, and efficient deep learning
  • BRI Media Framing - Comparative Analysis of Uzbekistan coverage
